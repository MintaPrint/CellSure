Questions

Straight answers.

How long does a test take?+

About 20-60 minutes per car, done where it's parked. Your certificate is issued the same day, usually in under an hour of the test.

Which EVs can you test?+

Most major makes: Nissan, Tesla, Kia, Hyundai, VW, BMW, Renault, MG and more, including plug-in hybrids. Tell us the exact model when you book and we'll confirm coverage before you pay anything.

How accurate is the result?+

We read the pack's own battery management system through the diagnostic port, the same data the manufacturer uses, then sense-check it against cell balance, fault codes and known baselines for that model. What we certify is the method: same instrument, same procedure, every car, run by a party with no stake in the sale.

What does it cost?+

A single pre-purchase test is from £99, certificate included. Dealers can get volume rates on multi-car visits.

Does the car need to be charged?+

No special prep. Anything above roughly 20% charge is fine. We'll flag it when you book if the model needs more.

Where do you cover?+

The East Midlands: Derbyshire, Nottinghamshire and Lincolnshire. Further afield by arrangement. Multi-car dealer visits usually justify the trip.
